# Client Journey - Pinnacle Events

## Stage 1: Initial Enquiry

### First Contact
- Response within 4 business hours (target)
- Enquiry qualification
- Initial needs assessment
- Budget range discussion
- Timeline feasibility check

### Discovery Call
- Understand event vision and objectives
- Discuss service options
- Explain our process
- Answer initial questions
- Determine fit for both parties

## Stage 2: Proposal and Booking

### Proposal Development
- Customised proposal based on requirements
- Service scope and inclusions
- Preliminary budget estimate
- Timeline overview
- Terms and conditions

### Booking Confirmation
- Signed agreement
- Deposit payment (typically 30%)
- Account setup
- Coordinator assignment
- Welcome pack and next steps

## Stage 3: Planning Phase

### Kick-off Meeting
- Detailed briefing with coordinator
- Vision and priorities discussion
- Key stakeholder identification
- Communication preferences
- Planning timeline agreement

### Ongoing Planning
- Regular check-ins (frequency based on timeline)
- Vendor shortlisting and booking
- Design and styling development
- Run sheet creation
- Budget tracking and updates

### Final Confirmations
- All vendor confirmations
- Final numbers and requirements
- Detailed run sheet sign-off
- Final payment
- Last-minute adjustments

## Stage 4: Event Delivery

### Event Week
- Final vendor briefings
- Site setup coordination
- Client communication
- Troubleshooting

### Event Day
- Early arrival and setup supervision
- Team briefing
- Run sheet execution
- Real-time problem solving
- Client liaison throughout
- Pack down and wrap-up

## Stage 5: Post-Event

### Immediate Follow-up (Within 48 hours)
- Thank you communication
- Initial feedback request
- Photo/content sharing

### Formal Review (Within 2 weeks)
- Detailed feedback survey
- Any issue resolution
- Testimonial request
- Case study discussion

### Ongoing Relationship
- Future event discussions
- Loyalty acknowledgement
- Periodic check-ins
- Referral appreciation

## Client Communication Standards

- Respond to all client communications within one business day
- Proactive updates at key milestones
- Clear and professional written communication
- Face-to-face or video meetings for important decisions
- Single point of contact (assigned coordinator)
- Escalation path available if needed (to Emma Wilson or Sophie Anderson)
