# Event Types and Services - Pinnacle Events

## Corporate Events

### Conferences and Seminars
- Half-day to multi-day formats
- 50 to 500+ attendees
- Full AV and technical support
- Speaker coordination and management
- Registration and attendee services
- Catering and hospitality

### Product Launches
- Brand activation events
- Media coordination
- Venue theming and styling
- Photography and videography coordination
- VIP guest management

### Corporate Functions
- Annual dinners and awards nights
- Team building events
- Client appreciation events
- Company milestones and celebrations
- Christmas parties and end-of-year functions

### AGMs and Shareholder Events
- Formal meeting coordination
- Voting and registration systems
- Catering for business audiences
- Strict timeline management

## Wedding and Social Events

### Weddings
- Full planning or day-of coordination
- Venue selection assistance
- Vendor coordination (catering, florals, photography, entertainment)
- Timeline and run sheet management
- Ceremony and reception coordination

### Private Celebrations
- Milestone birthdays
- Engagement parties
- Anniversary celebrations
- Cultural celebrations

## Government and Institutional

### Government Functions
- Formal protocol compliance
- Security coordination
- Media management
- Accessibility requirements
- Procurement process familiarity

### Institutional Events
- University functions
- Industry association events
- Charity galas and fundraisers

## Service Levels

### Full Service Planning
- Complete event management from concept to completion
- All vendor sourcing and coordination
- Budget management
- Creative direction and theming
- On-site management

### Partial Planning
- Client manages some elements
- We coordinate specific components
- Advisory and consulting support
- Vendor recommendations

### Day-Of Coordination
- Run sheet execution
- Vendor liaison on the day
- Problem-solving and troubleshooting
- Client can relax and enjoy their event

## Pricing Structure

Pinnacle Events pricing varies based on:
- Event type and complexity
- Guest count
- Service level required
- Venue and vendor costs (pass-through)
- Timing (peak vs off-peak season)

All quotes are customised to specific requirements. Initial consultations are complimentary.
