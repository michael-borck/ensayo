# Quality Standards - Pinnacle Events

## Our Quality Commitment

Every event we deliver reflects our reputation. We maintain consistent standards across all events regardless of size or budget.

## Service Standards

### Communication
- Respond to client enquiries within 4 business hours
- Proactive updates at every major milestone
- Clear, professional written communication
- Timely escalation of issues
- Post-event follow-up within 48 hours

### Planning
- Detailed briefing documentation for every event
- Written confirmation of all vendor bookings
- Run sheet provided minimum 7 days before event
- Budget tracking updated at every milestone
- Contingency planning for critical elements

### Execution
- Coordinator arrives minimum 2 hours before event start
- Full team briefing before guests arrive
- Run sheet adherence with flexibility for real-time needs
- Problem resolution without client burden
- Professional appearance and conduct

### Vendor Coordination
- Clear briefs provided to all vendors
- Confirmation calls/emails 48 hours before event
- On-site coordination and liaison
- Quality checks on all deliverables
- Performance feedback after events

## Quality Metrics

### Client Satisfaction
- Target: 4.5/5 average rating
- Current: 4.6/5 average
- Feedback collected for every event
- Issues addressed within 24 hours

### Event Delivery
- On-time start: 98% target
- Run sheet adherence: 95% target
- Zero critical failures: 100% target
- Client complaints resolved: 100%

### Repeat Business
- Repeat client rate: 40%+
- Referral rate: 60% of new business
- Client retention: Year-on-year growth

## Problem Resolution

### During Planning
- Identify issues early through regular check-ins
- Present solutions, not just problems
- Escalate to Operations Director if needed
- Document all changes and decisions

### During Events
- Coordinator empowered to make on-site decisions
- Client notified only of significant issues
- Focus on solutions, not blame
- Post-event review of any problems

### After Events
- Feedback reviewed within 48 hours
- Issues acknowledged immediately
- Resolution plan within 5 business days
- Follow-up to confirm satisfaction
- Process improvement implemented

## Continuous Improvement

- Post-event debrief for all major events
- Quarterly team review of feedback trends
- Annual vendor performance review
- Regular process documentation updates
- Team training and development
- Industry best practice adoption
