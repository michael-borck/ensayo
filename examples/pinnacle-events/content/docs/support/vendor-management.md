# Vendor Management - Pinnacle Events

## Preferred Vendor Network

Pinnacle Events maintains relationships with 50+ preferred vendors across all event categories. Preferred status means:
- Negotiated rates for our clients
- Priority booking during peak periods
- Consistent quality standards
- Reliable communication and service
- Regular performance reviews

## Vendor Categories

### Venues
- Hotels and conference centres
- Unique and boutique venues
- Outdoor and marquee sites
- Restaurant private dining
- Wineries and regional venues

### Catering
- Full-service caterers
- Specialist cuisines
- Dietary requirement specialists
- Mobile and food truck catering
- Beverage and bar services

### Audio Visual
- Conference AV specialists
- Lighting designers
- Sound and staging
- Video and livestreaming
- Technical directors

### Styling and Decor
- Florists
- Furniture and prop hire
- Linen and tableware
- Signage and branding
- Theming specialists

### Entertainment
- Live bands and DJs
- Speakers and MCs
- Performance artists
- Photo booths and activations
- Interactive experiences

### Photography and Video
- Event photographers
- Videographers
- Drone operators
- Live streaming services

## Vendor Selection Process

1. **Brief Development** - Understand client requirements
2. **Shortlisting** - Identify suitable vendors from our network
3. **Quotes** - Obtain competitive quotes
4. **Recommendation** - Present options with pros/cons
5. **Booking** - Secure confirmed vendor
6. **Coordination** - Manage all vendor communications
7. **Event Day** - On-site vendor management
8. **Review** - Post-event performance assessment

## Quality Standards

All preferred vendors must:
- Hold appropriate licences and insurance
- Demonstrate consistent service quality
- Communicate professionally and promptly
- Meet agreed timelines and specifications
- Resolve issues quickly and fairly
- Support Pinnacle's sustainability goals where possible

## Backup Planning

For every critical vendor category, we maintain:
- Primary vendor for the event
- Backup vendor on standby
- Emergency contact procedures
- Contingency equipment/supplies

This ensures events proceed smoothly even when unexpected issues arise.
