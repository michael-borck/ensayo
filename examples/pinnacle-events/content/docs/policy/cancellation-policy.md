# Cancellation and Postponement Policy

## Client Cancellations

### Deposit and Payment Structure
- **Booking deposit**: 30% of estimated total (non-refundable)
- **Interim payment**: 40% due 30 days before event
- **Final balance**: Due 7 days before event

### Cancellation Terms

#### More than 90 days before event
- Deposit retained by Pinnacle Events
- No additional charges
- Vendor deposits may apply (passed through at cost)

#### 60-90 days before event
- 50% of total estimated fee
- Vendor deposits and commitments passed through

#### 30-60 days before event
- 75% of total estimated fee
- All vendor commitments passed through

#### Less than 30 days before event
- 100% of total estimated fee
- All vendor commitments passed through

### Vendor Commitments
Vendors have their own cancellation policies. Once vendors are booked:
- Vendor deposits are non-refundable
- Cancellation charges from vendors are passed through at cost
- Pinnacle Events will negotiate on client's behalf where possible

## Postponement

### Postponement vs Cancellation
A postponement to a new date within 12 months may be treated more favourably than a cancellation, subject to:
- Venue and vendor availability for new date
- Minimal additional work required
- Agreement within 14 days of original cancellation notice

### Postponement Fees
- Administrative fee: $500-$1,500 depending on event size
- Vendor rescheduling fees (if any) passed through at cost
- Any price increases for new date apply

## Force Majeure

Events beyond reasonable control (natural disasters, pandemics, government restrictions) are handled case-by-case with client interests prioritised. Options typically include:
- Full postponement to new date
- Modified event format
- Partial refund of Pinnacle fees (vendor policies vary)

## Pinnacle Events Cancellation

In the unlikely event Pinnacle Events cannot fulfil a booking:
- Full refund of all Pinnacle fees
- Assistance finding alternative providers
- This has never occurred in our history

## Changes and Reductions

### Scope Changes
- Reductions in scope may still incur original fees if commitments made
- Increases in scope quoted separately
- All changes confirmed in writing

### Guest Number Reductions
- Final numbers confirmed 7-14 days before event (varies by vendor)
- Reductions after final confirmation may still incur full charges
- Catering guarantees are binding
