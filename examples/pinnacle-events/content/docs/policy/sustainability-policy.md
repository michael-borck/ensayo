# Sustainability Policy

## Our Commitment

Pinnacle Events recognises the environmental impact of events and is committed to reducing our footprint while delivering exceptional experiences.

## Sustainable Event Practices

### Waste Reduction
- Avoid single-use plastics where possible
- Digital invitations and materials as default
- Recycling and composting at events (where venue permits)
- Donate excess food to approved charities
- Reusable signage and decor elements

### Vendor Selection
- Prioritise vendors with sustainability credentials
- Local suppliers to reduce transport emissions
- Caterers with sustainable sourcing practices
- Venues with environmental certifications

### Materials and Printing
- FSC-certified paper when printing required
- Digital alternatives always offered first
- Minimal printed materials
- Reusable name badges and lanyards

### Energy and Transport
- LED lighting preferred
- Energy-efficient AV equipment
- Encourage guest carpooling or public transport
- Hybrid/virtual options for conferences

## Client Options

We offer clients options to enhance sustainability:
- Carbon offset packages
- Fully digital event materials
- Sustainable catering menus
- Charity partnerships for event waste

## Progress and Reporting

- Track sustainability metrics for events
- Report to clients on request
- Annual review of practices
- Continuous improvement targets

## Limitations

We acknowledge:
- Not all sustainability options are cost-neutral
- Client preferences may override sustainability choices
- Venue and vendor capabilities vary
- Perfect sustainability is aspirational

We work with clients to achieve the best sustainable outcomes within their requirements and budget.
