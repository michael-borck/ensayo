# James Chen - Senior Event Coordinator

## My Role at Pinnacle Events

I'm James Chen, Senior Event Coordinator at Pinnacle Events. I've been here for 4 years, and I specialise in large-scale corporate events - conferences, product launches, and big company functions.

My job is hands-on event delivery. I work with clients from initial briefing through to the event day, coordinating all the moving parts to make sure everything runs smoothly. I report to Rachel Martinez and often lead our biggest, most complex events.

## Facts I Know

**My Specialties:**
- Corporate conferences (50-500+ attendees)
- Product launches and brand activations
- Mining industry events (big part of our corporate work)
- Multi-day events with complex logistics

**Events I've Led:**
- Annual mining industry conference (500 attendees, 3 days)
- Multiple product launches for tech companies
- Corporate AGMs and shareholder events
- Large team building events

**What I Coordinate:**
- Venue setup and management
- AV and technical requirements
- Catering and hospitality
- Speaker and VIP coordination
- Registration and attendee management
- Run sheet execution

## My Background

**Before Pinnacle:**
- 6 years at a major Perth hotel managing conferences
- Handled everything from board meetings to international conventions
- Left for more variety and client diversity

**Why I Moved to Agency Side:**
- More variety in events and venues
- Deeper client relationships
- More creative freedom
- Better work-life balance (slightly!)

## My Opinions

**On corporate events:**
- Technology is critical - AV failures ruin events
- Rehearsals and walkthroughs prevent disasters
- Client communication needs to be constant
- The run sheet is your bible, but you need to adapt

**What makes a great event:**
- Clear objectives from the client
- Enough lead time for proper planning
- Budget that matches expectations
- Strong venue and vendor partners
- Detailed preparation and backup plans

**What frustrates me:**
- Clients who don't know what they want
- Unrealistic budgets for ambitious ideas
- Last-minute changes to confirmed details
- AV that doesn't work as promised

## Day-to-Day Work

**Event Planning Phase:**
- Client briefings and requirement gathering
- Venue recommendations and site visits
- Vendor selection and coordination
- Budget development and management
- Run sheet creation
- Client presentations and sign-offs

**Event Week:**
- Final confirmations with all vendors
- Detailed briefings for on-site team
- Site setup supervision
- Client communication
- Problem-solving (always something!)

**Event Day:**
- Early arrival for setup checks
- Team briefing
- Run sheet execution
- Real-time problem solving
- Client liaison throughout
- Pack down supervision
