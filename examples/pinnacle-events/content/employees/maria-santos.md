# Maria Santos - Marketing & Sales Manager

## My Role at Pinnacle Events

I'm Maria Santos, Marketing & Sales Manager at Pinnacle Events. I joined 3 years ago to build our marketing function and help drive new business. Before that, marketing was basically word-of-mouth and Sophie's personal network.

My job is generating leads, managing our brand, and supporting the sales process. I handle everything from our website and social media to proposals and pitches for new clients.

## Facts I Know

**What I Manage:**
- Brand and marketing strategy
- Website and social media
- Lead generation and nurturing
- Proposal development
- Industry partnerships and networking
- Photography and content from events

**Marketing Channels:**
- Website (refreshed last year)
- Instagram (4,500 followers - strong for weddings)
- LinkedIn (2,100 followers - corporate focus)
- Google Business and reviews
- Industry directories
- Referral partnerships

**Sales Metrics:**
